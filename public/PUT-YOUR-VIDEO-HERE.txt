Drop your intro video (.mp4) into THIS folder (public/).

Then open  src/config.js  and set:

    videoSrc: '/your-file-name.mp4'

Example: if you save it as  public/my-pitch.mp4
         set       videoSrc: '/my-pitch.mp4'

Tips:
- Keep it under ~50 MB if you can (compress with HandBrake) so it loads fast.
- An .mp4 with H.264 video + AAC audio plays everywhere.
- Optional: add a still image as a poster, e.g. public/poster.jpg, then set
  videoPoster: '/poster.jpg' in config.js.

You can delete this .txt file once your video is in.
